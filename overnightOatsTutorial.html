How to make overnight oats

You need these ingredients for overnight oats.

  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Oats

You can add special ingredients for more flavors

chocolate peanut butter berry
cocoa powder peanut butter strawberries
chocolate chips peanuts blueberries

Instructions:

  1. mix everything together
  2. refrigerate overnight
  3. enjoy!
